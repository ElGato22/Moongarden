Cat Therapy

If you're feeling stressed or anxious, spending time with cats can be a great way to relax and unwind. Our cat therapy sessions are designed to provide a calming and soothing environment where you can interact with our friendly felines and let your worries melt away.

Our cat therapy sessions are held in a private room, away from the main cafe area, to ensure a peaceful and serene atmosphere. During the session, you can spend time petting, playing with, and cuddling our cats, or simply sitting back and enjoying their company.

Our cat therapy sessions are available by appointment only, and are limited to one hour per session. To book a session, please fill out the form below and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
