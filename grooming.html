Cat Grooming

Keeping your cat clean and well-groomed is important for their health and happiness. At Moongarden Cat Cafe, we offer a range of grooming services to help your cat look and feel their best.

Services

  • Bath and blow-dry
  • Brushing and de-shedding
  • Nail trimming
  • Ear cleaning
  • Teeth cleaning

Pricing

Our pricing for cat grooming services is as follows:

  • Bath and blow-dry: £30
  • Brushing and de-shedding: £20
  • Nail trimming: £10
  • Ear cleaning: £15
  • Teeth cleaning: £25

Please note that pricing may vary depending on the size and breed of your cat. To book a grooming appointment, please call us at (555) 123-4567 or use the form below to send us a message.
