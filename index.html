About Us

Welcome to Moongarden Cat Cafe, where cat lovers come to enjoy delicious coffee and spend time with our adorable feline friends.

Our cafe is designed to be a cozy and relaxing space for both humans and cats. We have a dedicated play area where our resident cats can roam and play,

and customers are welcome to interact with them as much or as little as they like. Our cats are all rescues and have been carefully chosen for their friendly and sociable personalities.

We take the welfare of our cats very seriously, and we follow strict guidelines to ensure their health and happiness.

All of our cats receive regular veterinary care and are kept up-to-date with vaccinations and preventative treatments.

Our cafe also has strict hygiene protocols in place to ensure the health and safety of both cats and humans.

At Moongarden Cat Cafe, we believe that spending time with cats can be a great stress reliever and a mood booster.

We hope that our cafe can provide a peaceful retreat for our customers, and that our cats can bring a smile to your face.

So come on in, grab a coffee and a cat, and relax in our little corner of paradise..
